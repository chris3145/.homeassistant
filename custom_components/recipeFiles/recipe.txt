											<h1 class="gdlr-blog-title">Beefy Tomato Soup</h1>
						
					
					<div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info"><span class="gdlr-seperator">/</span><div class="blog-info blog-comments"><i class="icon-comments-alt"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/recipes/beefy-tomato-soup/#respond" >147</a></div><span class="gdlr-seperator">/</span><div class="blog-info blog-category"><i class="icon-folder-close-alt"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/recipes/main-dishes/" rel="tag">Main Dishes</a><span class="sep">,</span> <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/recipes/" rel="tag">Recipes</a><span class="sep">,</span> <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/recipes/soup/" rel="tag">Soup</a><span class="sep">,</span> <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/sponsored-posts/" rel="tag">Sponsored Posts</a></div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info gdlr-blog-full-date"><div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info"><div class="blog-info blog-date"><i class="icon-calendar-empty"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/2015/06/24/">24 Jun 2015</a></div><div class="clear"></div></div></div>

				<div class="gdlr-blog-content"><p class="p1">If you need a quick and comforting dinner that you&#8217;re whole family will love this Beefy Tomato Soup is what you&#8217;re looking for!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-topshot.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6290 size-full aligncenter" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-topshot.jpg" alt="Beef Tomato Soup is a total comfort dinner, full of pasta and beef in a creamy tomato broth!" width="500" height="730" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-topshot.jpg 500w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-topshot-205x300.jpg 205w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Soup is a regular meal in our house. Any time of the year, hot or cold, <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/recipes/homemade-turkey-soup/">winter</a> or <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/recipes/main-dishes/summer-grilled-chicken-soup/">summer</a>, we still make soup.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">It&#8217;s an easy one-pot meal that you can make ahead of time and have waiting for you for dinner. Add a loaf of bread or crackers and dinner is done. Check out how easy this is to make!</p>
<p> </p>
<div id="iwp2yaqx73to3s5fhrco" data-volume="70" data-ratio="16:9"></div>
<p><script type="text/javascript" src="//scripts.mediavine.com/videos/iwp2yaqx73to3s5fhrco.js" async="" data-noptimize=""></script></p>
<p>The recipe for this Beefy Tomato Soup literally happened when Christie and I were emptying out the fridge &#8211; trying to avoid going to the store AGAIN and using what we had on hand in the house. Cooking all the time for the web site means that I&#8217;m usually at some sort of grocery store on any given day of the week. Produce has to be fresh, things are forgotten or you need extra ingredients for do-overs.</p>
<p>But this time we weren&#8217;t going.</p>
<p>Even when we say that when we &#8220;need&#8221; to go to the store our refrigerator and freezer are packed to the doors. We&#8217;re always grabbing the staples when we&#8217;re out shopping at the grocery store or at <a href="http://www.walmart.com/search/?query=francesco%20rinaldi&amp;typeahead=francesco%20rinaldi" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Walmart</a>. I usually go up and down the aisles real quick and just grab whatever I think we need to keep on hand and pasta sauce is one of them.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-instore.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6274 aligncenter" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-instore.jpg" alt="beefy-tomato-instore" width="500" height="730" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-instore.jpg 500w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-instore-205x300.jpg 205w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>My little guy&#8217;s favorite kind of soup is tomato soup with basil &#8211; add a half a loaf of buttered bread and he&#8217;ll sit there all night. But for me, I like some sort of meat in soup &#8211; whatever it is &#8211; chicken, turkey, sausage or beef.</p>
<p>So I grabbed some ground beef from the freezer, cream cheese from the fridge, onions, basil and garlic from the pantry and got to work.</p>
<p>Usually we use crushed tomatoes to make tomato soup but this time we were out. It&#8217;s one of those things you always think you have&#8230;until you don&#8217;t. So instead I grabbed a jar of pasta sauce from the shelf. This recipe is just one of the ways I like to use spaghetti sauce <em>in</em> recipes as a short cut and a boost of flavor.</p>
<p>I started the soup by browning the meat with the onions and garlic.</p>
<p>Add in a few seasonings and then chicken stock and a whole jar or <a href="https://www.francescorinaldi.com/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Francesco Rinaldi</a> Traditional No Salt Added pasta sauce. Using the No Salt variety was perfect for this soup so that I could control the salt and seasonings that were going into it. Francesco Rinaldi is already made with the best quality tomatoes and fresh herbs so it&#8217;s perfect for a quick and delicious meal!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-pour.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6270 aligncenter" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-pour.jpg" alt="beefy-tomato-soup-pour" width="500" height="730" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-pour.jpg 500w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-pour-205x300.jpg 205w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>Give it a stir and simmer for 10 minutes.</p>
<p>Stir in some cream cheese to make it nice and thick, fresh basil and then pour in the pasta.</p>
<p><img class="size-full wp-image-6276 aligncenter" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-withpasta.jpg" alt="Beefy Tomato Soup is the perfect weeknight dinner, made quick with a few shortcuts!" width="500" height="730" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-withpasta.jpg 500w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-withpasta-205x300.jpg 205w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></p>
<p>Put the lid on a let the soup cook for another 10 minutes until the pasta is tender and dinner is served. We use this <a href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00008CM6I/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00008CM6I&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=mantitlemen09-20&amp;linkId=0cc037d3ccefcbaf682ddbcd75436367" target="_blank">Stainless Stockpot</a> all the time for our soups, sauces, and pasta dinners all the time!</p>
<p>Fresh basil gives this Beefy Tomato Soup a really amazing flavor &#8211; but don&#8217;t run out to grab it if you don&#8217;t have it, just substitute dried basil instead.</p>
<p><img class="size-full wp-image-6288 aligncenter" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-heroshot.jpg" alt="Beefy Tomato Soup is a total comfort dinner, full of pasta and beef in a creamy tomato broth!" width="512" height="768" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-heroshot.jpg 512w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/beefy-tomato-soup-heroshot-200x300.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 512px) 100vw, 512px" /></p>
<p>So many readers have commented that the noodles soak up all the broth if you want this soup the next day. Now some readers (including me) love this about this soup and some don&#8217;t. If you want the soup to keep overnight with more broth, cook the pasta separately and then add it to each bowl as you heat it up!</p>
